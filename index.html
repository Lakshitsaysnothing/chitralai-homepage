Simple Process

Three steps to effortless
media delivery

From event upload to attendee download — chitralai handles everything in between.

Organizer Uploads
Photographers or organizers upload all event photos and videos to the private chitralai album in one go.
AI Processes Media
Our face recognition AI scans every photo and video, tagging each person with precision and speed.
Attendee Scans QR
Event attendees scan a QR code with their phone, take a selfie, and instantly see only their own photos.
Download & Share
Attendees download their personalized album in seconds. No registration. No manual sharing needed.
